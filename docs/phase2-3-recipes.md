# Phase 2 & 3 Recipe Suggestions — 250 World Recipes

Curated list of recipes to consider adding across all 30+ cuisines. Prioritized for:
- **Coverage**: filling gaps in underrepresented cuisines

- **Dietary variety**: vegetarian, vegan, and gluten-free options throughout
- **Skill range**: Easy through Hard to serve all home cooks

Difficulty key: 🟢 Easy · 🟡 Medium · 🔴 Hard
